# External Patterns

Use this reference when explaining or evolving the planner. These are patterns
absorbed from external skill repositories, rewritten for eunomia.dev's public
technical-content posture.

## Repositories Reviewed

- `amplitude/builder-skills`: launch strategy, launch distribution, launch
  tweet, launch blog post, and launch metrics skills.
- `coreyhaines31/marketingskills` and
  `robertbstillwell/marketing-skills`: launch, social, content strategy,
  content repurposing, and owned/rented/borrowed channel patterns.
- `borghei/Claude-Skills`: launch tiers, readiness checks, social manager,
  content strategy, PMM checklists, and campaign metrics.
- `SpillwaveSolutions/running-marketing-campaigns-agent-skill`: campaign
  decision tree, UTM/measurement, social/media reference splitting, and
  validation checklists.
- `yabasha/copywrite-skill`: launch campaign phases, content calendar pillars,
  and post-launch FAQ/objection follow-up.
- `yoanbernabeu/producthunt-skills`: Product Hunt launch strategy, safe
  messaging, maker comment, launch-day checklist, analytics, and follow-up.
- `megastep/codex-skills`: blog repurposing workflow and platform-native
  derivative extraction.
- `blacktwist/social-media-skills`, `sergebulaev/x-skills`,
  `sergebulaev/linkedin-skills`, `jpeggdev/humanize-writing`, and
  `aaaronmiller/create-viral-content`: hook selection, thread/post structure,
  replies, anti-AI-tell editing, and human approval gates.
- `skainguyen1412/social-media-research-skill`: Reddit/X discovery, ranking,
  and evidence-grounded trend analysis.
- `stevenflanagan1/social-ai-team`: social workflow orchestration, content
  calendar, platform writers, publisher handoff, approval gates, and performance
  review.

## Patterns Absorbed

- Source extraction before writing: thesis, evidence, audience, proof, link,
  and reader outcome.
- Launch tiering: match effort to significance rather than treating every item
  as a full campaign.
- Platform matrix: choose channels by fit and make every variant native.
- Content atomization: one pillar artifact becomes multiple derivatives and
  micro-posts, each with a distinct angle.
- Owned/rented/borrowed channels: route attention back to durable owned assets
  while respecting platform communities.
- Duplicate/community scan before HN, Reddit, Lobsters, Product Hunt, or repeat
  posts.
- Approval gates before visible publishing, scheduling, voting, commenting, or
  messaging.
- Product Hunt safe messaging: ask for visits, comments, and feedback, never
  direct upvotes.
- First comment/pinned reply as its own surface for links, source notes, and
  context.
- Post-launch retro: comments and metrics update the next content plan.

## Patterns Rejected

- Autoposting, background platform APIs, mass DMs, and engagement automation.
- Upvote requests, vote incentives, or scripted community comments.
- Brittle algorithm hacks treated as universal rules.
- Pure SaaS conversion language that conflicts with our consulting/research
  posture.
- Private strategy, customer claims, pricing, or unreleased roadmap in public
  repo files.
